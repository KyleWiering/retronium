    Session Persistence Consent

    As a moderator, you can enable automatic session saving to your browser's local storage.

    Data stored locally includes:

    • Retrospective cards and groups
    • Votes and action items
    • Participant information (names and roles)
    • Session metadata (timestamps, phase)

    Privacy Notice:

    • Data is stored only in your browser
    • No data is sent to external servers
    • You can clear stored data at any time
    • Data expires after the retention period (default: 30 days)
